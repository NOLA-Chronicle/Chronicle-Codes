Roomba Boi

Description
Roomba Boi was developed initially to assist testing the development of other bots by clearing out discord server channel chats. His main functions were to 1) generate a desired number of messages and 2) delete all messages in the channel. Other bots do this more efficiently, however this was one of my first discord bots I developed.
