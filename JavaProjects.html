3-Segment Spirograph

Description
I worked on this project, as I did many others, in high school. I see it as a form of digital art, however the concept was not invented by myself. Spirographs create geometric drawings using sine and cosine formulas to plot points/lines on a coordinate plane. More info can be found here. They are also somewhat closely related to Fourier transforms, an advanced mathematical concept. More info on those can be found here.
